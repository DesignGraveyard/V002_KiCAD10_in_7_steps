# Description

Master KiCad 10 in just 7 steps! In this video tutorial, I will show you how to design your own PCB in KiCAD10.  
I will use a  ESP32-S3 USB development board from a previous video as example and guide you through the design process in 7 steps:  

* **Step 1:** Schematic Design & Workspace Setup
* **Step 2:** Component Selection & Footprint Assignment
* **Step 3:** Creating Custom Schematic Symbol (USB PCB Connector)
* **Step 4:** Designing Custom PCB Footprints
* **Step 5:** PCB Outline & Component Placement
* **Step 6:** Track Routing & Ground Copper Fills
* **Step 7:** Design Rule Check (DRC) & Exporting Gerbers  

Finally we will order, build & test the PCB.
